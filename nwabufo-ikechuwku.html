NWABUFO IKECHUKWU J.

(MANAGING PARTNER)

Ikechukwu is an experienced Attorney and Strong Business Development and Management Professional with a demonstrated History of working in the Legal and Financial Services Industries.

He has successfully resolved over 100 cases through Litigation, Arbitration and other Alternative Dispute Resolution Methods at various Tribunals and as a Chartered Company Secretary and Compliance Expert, he consults for and represents more than 200 cross-sectorial businesses, establishment and projects of varying sizes across Intellectual Property Protection, Corporate Governance and Regulatory Compliance, Credit and Risk Management, Tax Administration, Real Estate and Property Law, Immigration Law, Probate issues, Administrative and Labour Law.

EDUCATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUND

A Barrister, Solicitor and Notary Public of the Supreme Court of Nigeria and an Arbitrator of the Federal High Court Alternative Dispute Resolution Center, Ikechukwu was called to the Nigeria Bar in 2006 after obtaining an LLB (Hons) degree from the Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka and is currently undergoing processes to be a Solicitor of England and Wales and of Canada.

Ikechukwu is an LLM Candidate (International Commercial Law) at the University of Salford Manchester United Kingdom and an MBA Candidate (Finance & Investment Specialization) at the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, and holds a Diploma in French Language (DELF-B1) from the Centre International d’Etudes Pedagogique of the Republic of France Ministry of Education; a Post-Graduate Certificate in Public Policy Economics from the University of Oxford; a Certificate in Public Financial Management from the Association of Certified Chartered Accountants (ACCA); a Post-Graduate Diploma in Global Governance from the International Business Management Institute Berlin Germany as well as Post-Graduate Certificates in Regulatory Compliance (University of Pennsylvania USA), Global Diplomacy (University of London), Arbitration of International Disputes (Leiden University, Netherlands), Investigation and Prosecution of International Crimes (Leiden University, Netherlands), Project Management (University of Adelaide, Australia) and Introduction to the Patent Cooperation Treaty (World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) Academy).

He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Secretaries of Nigeria (ICSAN), a Fellow of the Institute of Administrative Management (IAM) UK, a Member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN), Member of the Institute of Credit Administration (ICA) an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (CIARB) UK and a Member of the Association of Professional Negotiators and Mediators (APNM). He speaks English and French fluently.
