OKPALAELECHI IKECHUKWU P.

(SENIOR PARTNER, ARBITRATION LEAD)

A graduate of Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Ikechukwu II is a certified member of the Association of Professional Negotiators and Mediators and was called to the Bar in 2006.He served meritoriously in the Ibadan branch of Ecobank Plc from 2006-2007 before returning to his professional passion.
