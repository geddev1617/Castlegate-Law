MOLOKWU DANIEL U. (SENIOR PARTNER, HEAD REAL ESTATE & CONVEYANCING)

Daniel became a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria in 2006. A Notary Public of the Supreme Court of Nigeria, He oversees the Real Estate and Conveyancing Department of Castlegate Legal Consults and is also an expert in Mortgages and Loans servicing, debt rescheduling and recovery, with a reputation for thoroughness at corporate investigations.

Daniel is a Masters-of-Business Administration (MBA) Candidate from the Ahmadu Bello University holds a Post-Graduate Diploma in Political Science from the University of Abuja, a Post-Graduate Diploma in Global Governance from the International Business Management Institute Berlin Germany, a Post-Graduate Diploma in Basic Computer Skills from the Scholars International Institute of Technology, a Post-Graduate Diploma in Project Management from the International Business Management Institute Berlin Germany, a Diploma in French Language (DELF-A1, Republic of France Ministry of Education Centre International d’Etudes Pedagogique), and Post-Graduate Certificates in the Art of Negotiation from the University of California, Irvine; Mediation and Conflict Resolution from the prestigious ESSEC Business School, Introduction to Patent Cooperation Treaty from (World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) Academy), in Team Development from the Nordic Business and Finance Institute Sweden, in Intellectual Property and Regulatory Compliance from the University of Pennsylvania United States and in Financial Markets from the Universite de Geneve Switzerland. He speaks English, French, and Igbo.
