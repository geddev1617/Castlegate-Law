WAWU NORI

(SENIOR PARTNER, HEAD OF CORPORATE AND BUSINESS LAW)

Nori Wawu was called to the bar in 2006, after obtaining her LL.B (Hons.) from the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University (ABU) Zaria and B.L from the Nigerian Law School Bwari. She is an Appointed Neutral Facilitator of the Federal High Court Alternative Dispute Resolution Centre and is a Member of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) and the Association of Professional Negotiators and Mediators (APNM). She holds Post-Graduate Certificates in Crime, Justice and Society from the University of Sheffield, and in Maritime: an introduction to Shipping Transactions from the Law Society of Scotland, among others. Joining from the Firm of Ado, Wawu & Partners, she is the Head of Corporate and Business Law Department of the firm and also consults on Immigration and International Commercial transactions. She also consults as an Immigration and International Commercial Lawyer and speaks English, French, Hausa and Fulani with native proficiency.
