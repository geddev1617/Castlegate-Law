UCHE STANLEY ALLISON. (HEAD OF PRACTICE LAGOS, SENIOR PARTNER)

With over a decade of post qualification experience, Uche has advised on company formation, strategy, mergers and acquisitions, corporate lending transactions, international finance and banking, transactional corporate law, e-commerce, logistics and Supply chain, taxation, contract management, civil litigation, arbitration and capital market investments amongst others. He worked as an Assistant Senior Legal Officer at BUA Group where he had oversaw and rendered legal support to Thirteen (13) subsidiaries within the Group and advised on multi-million Dollar mergers and acquisitions, corporate lending transactions, immigration issues with respect to the Group’s expatriates amongst others. Uche graduated from the University of Abuja, Nigeria in 2005 and was called and enrolled as a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria in 2007.

He is currently nearing completion of his studies for a Master Business Administration in Logistics and Supply Chain (MBA) at the Malaysia University of Science and Technology. He also possesses a Master of Laws (LL.M) Degree in International Finance and Banking Law from the University of Liverpool, United Kingdom. Uche Allison is a member in good standing of the Nigerian Bar Association.
